Georgetown University

Founded in 1789, Georgetown University is a prestigious private research university located in Washington, D.C.
It is the oldest Catholic and Jesuit institution of higher education in the United States, known for its rigorous academics, global perspective, and commitment to social justice.

Courses We Offer

Georgetown University offers customizable, interdisciplinary programs with a global focus to prepare students for leadership roles across industries.

Intermediate

Offers programs bridging high school and higher education, including Summer Pre-College Programs, Foundation and Bridge Programs for academic skill-building, and Dual Enrollment for earning college credits.

Degree

Offers a wide range of degree courses, including undergraduate programs in arts, sciences, business, foreign service, and health sciences; graduate degrees in law, business, public policy, and medicine; and professional programs in leadership, global affairs, and more.

Post graduation

Offers advanced degrees in fields such as law, business, public policy, medicine, international relations, and more. With a focus on interdisciplinary learning, global engagement, and professional development, these programs prepare graduates for leadership roles and specialized careers worldwide.

Our Global Campus

Georgetown University has a global presence with campuses and programs in key cities, including Washington, London and New York. These global campuses offer students opportunities to engage in international studies, internships, and cross-cultural experiences, enhancing their education with a global perspective.

Our facilities

Georgetown University offers state-of-the-art facilities, including a vast library with extensive research resources, modern cafeterias providing diverse dining options, and recreational spaces such as sports fields and playgrounds. These amenities support both academic and extracurricular activities, fostering a vibrant campus life.

World class library

A modern library with all facilities is a hub of knowledge and innovation, offering an extensive collection of books, e-books, journals, and multimedia resources. It provides seamless digital access with computers, high-speed Wi-Fi, and online databases, catering to the needs of tech-savvy users.

Largest playground

A dedicated space for students to engage in sports, fitness, and recreational activities. It typically features facilities like open fields for football and cricket, courts for basketball, volleyball, and tennis, as well as tracks for running and athletics.

Tasty and healthy food

A lively space where students gather to enjoy meals, snacks, and beverages. It offers a variety of affordable and nutritious food options, catering to diverse tastes and dietary preferences.
